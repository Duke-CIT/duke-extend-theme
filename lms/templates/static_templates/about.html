Duke Extend provides Duke faculty and staff the ability to offer online courses or modules to anyone in or outside the university. With Duke Extend you can:

Build and deliver flexible content

Deliver full online courses or smaller, modular content. Offer content on-demand or around your schedule. Teach your students, school, or community.

Reach global audiences of any size

Duke Extend is built to grow, helping you expand your reach and address global challenges for communities across the world.

Get support that meets your teaching needs

Duke Extend runs on the open source Open edX platform and can be customized to meet your specific course needs. Courses or modules can integrate with other Duke learning tools and Duke NetID or OneLink authentication.
