Duke Extend: Duke Open Online Courses

Duke Extend provides Duke faculty and staff with a way to offer online modules to anyone from small, Duke-affiliated groups to the entire world. Duke Extend gives you the ability to:

  • Build and deliver flexible content

    Use Duke Extend to deliver full online courses or smaller, modular content. Courses can be offered “on-demand” (always available to students) or “session-based” (run for a limited time).

  • Get support that meets your teaching needs

    Duke University operates Duke Extend, using the Open edX technology developed by MIT and Harvard University. Duke Extend can be customized to meet your specific course needs and integrates with other Duke learning tools, such as the Warpwire video platform. Instructors can manage users and groups through the Duke NetID authentication system.

  • Reach global audiences of any size

    Duke Extend courses can enroll large learner groups at or beyond Duke.
